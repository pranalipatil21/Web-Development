About ACM-W

Welcome to the ACM-W (Association for Computing Machinery - Women) . We are dedicated to supporting and advocating for women in computing and related fields.Established in 2019. This chapter has been established with an aim to support and motivate the women in computing, and thereby bring about gender equality in this field.

Glimpse of the Induction Program

Our induction program introduces new members to ACM-W's values and initiatives. It includes workshops, networking events, and opportunities to meet our leadership team.
